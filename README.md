# Citronom Emulator

## Overview
Citronom is an advanced emulator designed to replicate the functionality of the popular Citra Emulator, specifically targeting Nintendo 3DS games. It offers gamers the ability to experience 3DS titles on various platforms with enhanced performance and visual fidelity.

## Features
- **High Performance:** Citronom leverages cutting-edge optimization techniques to deliver smooth gameplay and efficient resource usage.
- **Compatibility:** It aims to support a wide range of 3DS games, allowing players to enjoy their favorite titles without issues.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** The emulator provides an intuitive interface, making it easy for users of all experience levels to navigate and configure settings.
- **Multiplayer Support:** Like Citra, Citronom includes features for online multiplayer gaming, enabling gamers to connect and play with friends remotely.
- **Additional Enhancements:** Users can find various enhancements, such as improved graphics, customizable controls, and mod support.

## Relationship to Citra
Citronom is built upon the foundational technology of the Citra Emulator, which is known for its reliability and extensive development history. By incorporating the lessons learned from Citra, Citronom aims to provide a more refined and enhanced gaming experience, enhancing the capabilities offered by its predecessor.

Citronom continues to evolve, driven by an active community and dedicated developers committed to expanding its features and compatibility. Whether you’re reliving nostalgic moments or discovering new games, Citronom is designed to make your gaming experience enjoyable and accessible.
